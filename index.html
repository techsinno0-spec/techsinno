About TECHSINNO

ENGINEERS.
BUILDERS.
SOLVERS.

TECHSINNO (Pty) Ltd is a registered South African engineering company specialising in electronics repair, industrial automation, and agricultural IoT.

Led by a practising mechatronics and electronics engineer, TECHSINNO combines hands-on hardware expertise with modern cloud and software integration.

Operations are headquartered in Kuilsriver, Western Cape, with planned expansion into Kinshasa, DRC.
