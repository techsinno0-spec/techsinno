Overview

Automate, integrate, and maintain

TECHSINNO delivers industrial automation and maintenance services for manufacturing, processing, and laboratory environments. From programming PLCs and integrating SCADA systems to maintaining and repairing plant machinery, we keep your operation running.

We work with major PLC and HMI platforms and understand the realities of industrial environments — downtime is expensive, so our solutions prioritise reliability, clear documentation, and maintainability. We service both new automation projects and existing equipment.
