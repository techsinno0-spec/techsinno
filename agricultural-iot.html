AGRICULTURAL IoT

Smart sensor networks for modern farming — monitor soil, climate, and irrigation in real time from anywhere.

Overview

Your farm, connected and data-driven

TECHSINNO designs and deploys end-to-end agricultural IoT systems — custom sensor networks that transmit live field data to a cloud dashboard. Monitor soil moisture, temperature, pH, and irrigation status without walking the fields.

Our systems use rugged, solar-powered sensor nodes built for remote locations, connected through Azure IoT for reliable data and instant alerts. Get an SMS or email the moment a threshold is breached — so you act before a problem becomes a loss.

What we offer

Our Agricultural IoT Services

Soil Monitoring

Real-time soil moisture, temperature, and pH measurement across your fields.

Climate Sensing

Air temperature, humidity, and light monitoring for crop conditions.

Irrigation Control

Monitor and automate irrigation based on live soil data.

Cloud Dashboard

Access all your farm data from any device, anywhere, anytime.

Real-time Alerts

SMS and email alerts the moment thresholds are breached.

Solar-Powered

Self-sufficient nodes for fields without mains power.
