PCB DESIGN & ENGINEERING

Professional printed circuit board design — from schematic capture to manufacturing-ready files, built to industry standards.

Overview

Manufacturing-ready PCBs, designed right

TECHSINNO provides end-to-end PCB design services — we take your circuit from schematic through layout to a complete manufacturing package. Whether it's a simple 2-layer board or a complex multi-layer design, we deliver clean, verified, production-ready files.

Every design goes through rigorous design rule checks (DRC) and electrical rule checks (ERC) before release, minimising costly manufacturing errors. We output industry-standard Gerber and drill files compatible with any PCB fabricator.

What we offer

Our PCB Design Services

Schematic Capture

Clean, well-organised schematics with proper annotation and net labelling.

PCB Layout

Optimised component placement and routing for signal integrity and manufacturability.

DRC / ERC Checks

Design and electrical rule verification to catch errors before manufacturing.

Gerber Output

Complete manufacturing package — Gerbers, drill files, BOM, and pick-and-place.

Multi-layer Design

2, 4, or more layers with proper stackup and impedance control.

Design for Manufacture

Layouts optimised to reduce cost and improve yield at the fabricator.
