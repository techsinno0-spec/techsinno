ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

From concept to working hardware — end-to-end electronics engineering services for industrial, commercial, and agricultural applications.

Overview

Complete electronics solutions, start to finish

TECHSINNO offers full-cycle electronics engineering — we take your idea from initial consultation through design, prototyping, building, installation, and ongoing maintenance. Whether you need a custom control board, a sensor system, or repair of existing industrial electronics, we cover the entire lifecycle.

Led by a qualified mechatronics and electronics engineer, every project combines hands-on hardware skill with rigorous engineering practice. We work with businesses across South Africa and the DRC, providing reliable, practical solutions that perform in real-world conditions.

What we offer

Our Electronics Services

Consultation

Design
